## Executive Summary

2026 represents a critical inflection point for NexusAI. With Series B funding (expected Q1), we will accelerate our path from promising startup to market leader in AI-powered supply chain intelligence for the mid-market. Our strategy focuses on three pillars: Product Leadership, Market Expansion, and Operational Excellence.

### Pillar 2: Market Expansion

**Goal:** Triple ARR from $8.6M to $28M while expanding geographic footprint.

**Key Initiatives:**

1. **Enterprise Acceleration**
   - Target: 60% of new ARR from Enterprise segment
   - Dedicated enterprise sales team (4 AEs)
   - Strategic account management program
   
2. **International Expansion**
   - UK market entry (Q1)
   - Germany market entry (Q2)
   - Target: 15% of new ARR from international by Q4
   
3. **Channel Development**
   - 2-3 strategic partnerships (consultancies, integrators)
   - Referral partner program launch
   
4. **Vertical Go-to-Market**
   - Healthcare-specific messaging and case studies
   - Manufacturing industry events presence

**Success Metrics:**
- ARR: $28M (225% growth)
- Enterprise mix: 60%+ of new ARR
- International: 15%+ of new ARR
- Net Revenue Retention: 115%+

### Pillar 3: Operational Excellence

**Goal:** Build the operational foundation to support 3x growth while maintaining efficiency.

**Key Initiatives:**

1. **Team Scaling**
   - Grow from 42 to 85 FTEs
   - Key hires: VP Engineering, VP CS, Sales Directors
   - Maintain culture through hypergrowth
   
2. **Infrastructure & Compliance**
   - EU data center (Q1)
   - SOC 2 Type II certification
   - GDPR full compliance
   
3. **Customer Success at Scale**
   - Implement digital CS for Business tier
   - Reduce time-to-value to <8 weeks
   - Customer health scoring automation
   
4. **Financial Operations**
   - Series B close (Q1)
   - Implement FP&A tooling
   - Board reporting automation

**Success Metrics:**
- Gross margin: 82%+
- CAC payback: <10 months
- Employee NPS: 60+
- Customer implementation time: <8 weeks

## Risk Factors

### High Priority Risks

1. **Series B Timing**
   - Risk: Delay beyond Q1 impacts all initiatives
   - Mitigation: Parallel conversations with 3 firms, bridge option available

2. **Enterprise Sales Execution**
   - Risk: Longer cycles, fewer reps with enterprise experience
   - Mitigation: Hire experienced enterprise AEs, sales enablement investment

3. **Product Delivery**
   - Risk: Supplier risk module delays impact competitive position
   - Mitigation: Dedicated team, clear scope, no feature creep

### Medium Priority Risks

4. **International Complexity**
   - Risk: Underestimate effort for UK/EU market entry
   - Mitigation: Phase approach, local partnerships

5. **Team Culture**
   - Risk: Hypergrowth dilutes culture
   - Mitigation: Values-based hiring, culture initiatives
