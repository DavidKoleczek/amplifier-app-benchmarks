# Leadership Scheduling Preferences

*Notes compiled by EA team - January 2026*

## General Guidelines

- **Leadership offsites** should be scheduled for 90 minutes minimum to allow for meaningful strategic discussion
- **Preferred days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday (avoid Monday/Friday when possible)
- **Preferred times**: Mid-morning (10-12) or early afternoon (1-3pm) tend to work best
- **Location preference**: On-site in the Boardroom or Cascade Conference Room if possible

## Scheduling Tips

- Book 30 minutes early to allow for pre-meeting prep/small talk
- Always send calendar invite with video link even for in-person (backup)
- Include agenda in calendar invite body
- For cross-timezone attendees, note timezone in invite title
