## Resource Requirements

### Recruiting Support
- Dedicated recruiter: 0.5 FTE for Q1-Q2
- Recruiting budget: $15,000 (job boards, LinkedIn Recruiter)
- Referral bonus budget: $15,000 ($5,000 per hire)

### Interview Team Commitment
- Estimated interview hours: 120 hours total across team
- Team members involved: 6 (all senior+ engineers)
- Impact on sprint velocity: ~10% reduction in Q1

### Onboarding Support
- Buddy assignments for each new hire
- Structured 30-60-90 day plans
- Hardware/equipment budget: $4,500 ($1,500 per hire)

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
