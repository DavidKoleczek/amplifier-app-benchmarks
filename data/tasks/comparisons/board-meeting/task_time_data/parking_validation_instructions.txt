PARKING VALIDATION INSTRUCTIONS
NexusAI Office - 100 California Street, San Francisco
=====================================================

FOR BOARD MEMBERS AND VISITORS
------------------------------

We validate parking at the following garage:

ONE CALIFORNIA GARAGE
Address: 100 California Street (enter on Davis Street)
Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM daily

VALIDATION PROCESS:
1. Park in the garage (any level)
2. Bring your ticket to the NexusAI reception desk (5th floor)
3. We will stamp your ticket for up to 4 hours free parking
4. Additional hours charged at garage rate ($8/hour)

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Validation must be done BEFORE you leave
- Lost tickets charged at $45 flat rate
- Oversized vehicles (trucks, SUVs over 6'6") use Level P1 only
- Electric vehicle charging available on P2 (Tesla, ChargePoint)

ALTERNATIVE: VALET
If you prefer valet parking:
- Valet stand is at main lobby entrance
- Cost: $35 flat rate (we do NOT validate valet)
- Recommended for board members arriving from airport
