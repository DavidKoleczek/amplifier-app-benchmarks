EMPLOYEE HEADSHOT UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
=====================================

HR Portal > My Profile > Photo Upload

REQUIREMENTS:
- File format: JPEG or PNG
- Minimum size: 400x400 pixels
- Maximum size: 5MB
- Background: Plain, neutral preferred
- Attire: Business casual or professional

TIPS FOR A GOOD HEADSHOT:
1. Face the camera directly
2. Ensure good lighting (natural light works best)
3. Choose a plain background
4. Wear solid colors (avoid busy patterns)
5. Smile naturally!

APPROVAL PROCESS:
Photos are reviewed by HR within 2 business days. You'll receive an email when your photo is approved and live in the directory.
