HOW TO USE THESE VIDEOS
================================================================================

IN EMAIL OUTREACH:
- Use short clips (under 90 sec) in prospecting emails
- Match industry to prospect's industry
- Example: "Here's how a similar company transformed their forecasting [link]"

IN SALES DECKS:
- Embed full testimonials in presentation
- One video per key claim you're making
- Let customer speak for you on differentiators

IN SOCIAL SELLING:
- Share clips on LinkedIn with commentary
- Tag relevant prospects (carefully)
- Use for credibility building

IN PROPOSALS:
- Include 1-2 relevant customer stories
- Link to full video for those who want more
- Match to prospect's key concerns

- Enterprise customers
- Quotable results (specific numbers)
