HORIZONWORKS EMPLOYEE RESOURCE GROUPS (ERGs)
=============================================
Last Updated: December 2025

All employees are welcome to join any ERG as members or allies. ERGs receive 
company funding for events and activities. ERG leads receive additional 
recognition in performance reviews.

INTERESTED IN STARTING A NEW ERG?

Contact the DEI team at dei@horizonworks.com. Requirements:
- Minimum 20 interested members
- Executive sponsor commitment
- Proposed charter and leadership structure
- Alignment with company DEI goals

Currently exploring: Asian employee ERG, Parents/Caregivers ERG, First-Gen ERG

------------------------------------------------------------------------

ERG PARTICIPATION IS:
✓ Voluntary and open to all
✓ Supported with company time (4 hours/month)
✓ Recognized in performance reviews for leads
✓ Funded for events and activities

ERG PARTICIPATION IS NOT:
✗ Required for any employee
✗ A substitute for systemic change
✗ Limited to identity group members (allies welcome!)
