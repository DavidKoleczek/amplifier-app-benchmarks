## Key Market Trends

1. **Compensation Inflation:** Tech salaries increased 8-12% YoY in Seattle market
2. **Remote Premium Gone:** Remote roles now pay equivalent to local market
3. **DevOps/Platform Premium:** 10-15% premium over general SWE roles
4. **Retention Risk:** Below-market compensation correlates with 2x turnover
