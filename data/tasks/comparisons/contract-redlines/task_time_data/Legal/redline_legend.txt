REDLINE DOCUMENT LEGEND
=======================

This document explains the markup conventions used in our contract review process.

CHANGE MARKERS IN TEXT:

[DELETED]    - Language that has been removed from the original
[ADDED]      - New language inserted that was not in original
[MODIFIED]   - Language that has been changed (see before/after)
[NEW SECTION] - Entirely new section added to the agreement

CHANGE CATEGORIES:

The changes fall into the following priority categories:

CRITICAL (RED) - Must be accepted; dealbreakers if rejected
- Liability cap modifications (Section 6.2)
- Indemnification scope changes (Section 7.1, 7.2)
- IP ownership and data usage restrictions (Section 4)

IMPORTANT (ORANGE) - Strongly preferred; negotiate hard
- Insurance limit increases (Section 8)
- Breach notification timing (Section 9.3)
- Termination flexibility (Section 2.2, 2.4, 2.5)

STANDARD (YELLOW) - Best practice improvements
- Payment term extensions (Section 3.2, 3.3, 3.4)
- Audit rights (Section 9.4, 9.5)
- Confidentiality survival period (Section 5.3)

NICE TO HAVE (GREEN) - Can concede if needed for deal
- Service level additions (Section 1.4)
- Dispute resolution procedures (Section 10.2)
- Survival clause (Section 10.5)
